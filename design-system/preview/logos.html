Clear space

Keep at least the trunk's base height clear on every side. The wordmark divider in the rectangle lockup is the minimum.

Min size

Square: 64px tall. Rectangle: 180px wide. Below that, use the mark alone (favicon).

Don't

Recolor it, drop-shadow it, or place it on a busy photograph without first sitting it inside a glass tile.
